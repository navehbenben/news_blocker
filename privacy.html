Privacy Policy – News Blocker

News Blocker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. This extension operates entirely on your local browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No cookies
  • No external API calls

Your browsing activity stays private and local to your machine.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer via the project’s GitHub repository.
